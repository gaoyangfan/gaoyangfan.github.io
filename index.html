I am currently a Computational Biologist in the Computer Aided Rejuvenation group, working with Simone Bianco. Before joining Altos, I worked with Krešimir Josić at the University of Houston and the Bennett Lab at Rice University as a postdoc on combining both mathematical and synthetic biological tools to understand emergent phenomena, such as pattern formation arise from expanding bacterial colony and cell-cell communication in heterogeneous environment.
